What is Organ Donation?

Organ donation is a generous act where a person allows their organs or tissues to be used to save lives. It can be done during life or after death and can include kidneys, liver, heart, lungs, and more.

Live Donation

Healthy individuals can donate one kidney, a part of the liver, or bone marrow while still alive.

Deceased Donation

Organs and tissues can be donated after brain death or cardiac death, saving multiple lives.

Who Can Donate?

Almost anyone can become a donor. Medical conditions are reviewed before approval.

Why Should You Donate?

💖 Save Lives

Save up to 8 lives through organ donation. One donor can transform multiple lives.

🧬 Improve Quality

Help improve quality of life through tissue donation — vision, mobility, and more.

🔁 Second Chances

Give a second chance at life to those suffering from chronic organ failure.
