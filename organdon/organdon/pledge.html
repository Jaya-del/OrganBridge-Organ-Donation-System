Why Pledge?

Pledging your organs is a simple procedure. Once you fill out and submit this form, we will send you a donor card along with a unique government registration number. Please note that this card is an expression of intent and does not carry legal weight.

According to Indian law, your next of kin will decide on organ donation upon your death. Therefore, it is very important that you discuss your decision with your family. By pledging to donate your organs, you are taking an important step to help save lives.

Join us in making a difference. Your pledge today could bring hope and relief to many families in need.
