A Teenage Angel

The touching story of Arya, a 16-year-old who became a hero through organ donation.

By OrganBridge Team · March 19, 2025

Arya was a vibrant 16-year-old with a heart full of dreams and compassion. She believed in helping others and had even convinced her parents to let her register as an organ donor.

Tragedy struck when Arya met with a fatal accident while on her way to school. Her parents, devastated by the loss, honored her wish to donate her organs.

“We lost our daughter, but through her, five lives were saved. That brings us some peace.” — Arya’s father

Her organs went on to save a child with a failing heart, a mother awaiting a liver transplant, and three others in desperate need of kidneys and corneas. Arya's selflessness became a shining light in the darkest hour for several families.

Her legacy lives on — not just in those she saved, but in the countless others inspired by her story.
