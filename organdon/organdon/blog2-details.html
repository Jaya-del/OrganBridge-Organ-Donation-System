My Husband, My Hero

A powerful story of love, courage, and the life-saving gift of living organ donation.

By OrganBridge Team · March 14, 2025

Sneha, a 35-year-old software engineer, had been battling liver cirrhosis for years. When her condition worsened, doctors told her she would not survive without a liver transplant.

Her husband, Ravi, didn’t hesitate for a moment. He volunteered to donate a portion of his liver. After rigorous testing and preparation, the transplant was carried out successfully.

“He saved my life, not with roses or poems, but with a part of his own body. I owe him everything.” — Sneha

The couple has now become active advocates for organ donation. Ravi’s act of love not only saved his wife, but also inspired many in their community to register as donors.

Living organ donations are possible and incredibly impactful. It takes one courageous decision to rewrite someone’s future.
